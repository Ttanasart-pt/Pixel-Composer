Gradient node draw gradient on to the surface. Visit <node gradient_out/> for a node that output a 
gradient data.

<img gradient_types/>

## Properties

### <junc gradient/>

The gradient to draw.

### <junc shift/>

Shift the gradient keys by a fix amount.

<img gradient_shift/>

### <junc scale/>

Scale the gradient.

<img gradient_scale/>

### <junc loop/>

Loop the keyframe back when overflowing.

<img gradient_loop/>

### <junc type/>

Types of the gradient.

<img gradient_types/>

### <junc angle/>

<span class="inline-code">Linear</span> and <span class="inline-code">Radial</span> types only. The angle of the 
pattern.

### <junc radius/>

<span class="inline-code">Circular</span> type only. Radius of the pattern.

### <junc center/>

Center point of the pattern.

### <junc uniform ratio/>

For non-square output, whether to follows the surface aspect ratio or use uniform 1:1 ratio.

<img gradient_uniform_ratio/>
