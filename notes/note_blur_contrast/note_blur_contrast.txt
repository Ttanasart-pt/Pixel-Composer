Contrast blur node blur out area with low contrast. This can be use to reduce the amount of detail in the image. 
Use this node with <node dither/> to convert random noise into a pattern.

<img-deco blur_contrast caption="Credit: Synth Cities Environment by ansimuz">>

## Properties

### <junc size/>

Blur radius.

### <junc threshold/>

The amount of contrast until the blur stop.
