Shape map node map surface into different polygon shapes.


<img-deco shape_map>



## Properties


### <junc shape/>


Shape of the mapping, between circle or polygon.


### <junc sides/>


<span class="bold">Polygon</span> shape, the amount of sides.


### <junc scale/>


The size of the mapped shape.


### <junc angle/>


Shape angle


### <junc map scale/>


Repeat surface in each axis.
