Glow node apply glow effect to the edge of the image. What consider an edge is depends on the <junc mode/>.


<img-deco glow>



## Properties


### <junc mode/>


Whether to apply glow effect on the transparent pixel or the darker pixel.


### <junc size/>


Size of the glow.


### <junc strength/>


Glow intensity.


### <junc color/>


Color of the glow, note that the glow is added in additively.


### <junc draw original/>


Whether to draw the original surface, or glow only.
